Project: Sleep Questionnaire Application
Client: Danny Lewin

1. Project Overview
Helvetica Lake will develop a web-based interactive questionnaire focused on sleep health and
sleep disorders. The application will:
●

Provide patients with a guided, validated questionnaire.

●

Generate a narrative report with tailored guidance, educational links, and references to
clinicians and publicly available resources.

●

Offer a freemium experience that only collects de-identified demographic data (year of
birth, county/zip code, sex).

●

Output recommendations for either sleep health optimization or probable diagnoses
(non-treatment) across a defined set of sleep disorders.
